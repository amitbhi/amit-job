Amit Vishnu Bhise is an Artificial Intelligence Leader and Technologist with 20 years of progressive experience in IT, including 11 years in Data Science and 7 years in Generative AI. 

Currently, he is the AGM / Lead Data Scientist (Operating at GM Scope) at Suzlon Energy Ltd (since March 2025). In this role, he partners with GM leadership to co-own enterprise AI, data science, and data engineering strategy across a ₹9 Cr (€0.87M) portfolio, and drives execution across a 40-member federated team. His achievements include leading development of an LLM-enabled analytics ecosystem integrating SAP ECC and HANA datasets with Redis cache architecture, leading enterprise AI governance and security practices (NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001, MITRE ATLAS), reducing unplanned downtime by 8% using predictive maintenance, and overseeing full Snowflake integration and project migrations. He also launched production-grade Gen-AI systems and has brief exposure to SAP HANA and Salesforce implementation.

Previously, he spent nearly a decade at Tata Consultancy Services (2015-2024), where he served as a Process Associate and Data Science Lead. He worked with major clients like Telstra, Morgan Stanley, and the Rockefeller Foundation, leading teams in Snowflake onboarding, RPA, and Gen-AI assistant development.

Earlier in his career, he held roles at Hutchison Whampoa / 3 Global Services and Wipro Ltd, gaining foundational experience in technical support and network operations.

Amit is a published researcher with works in "Behaviour & Information Technology" (Scopus Indexed) and the "XYz Journal". His research interests include personal longitudinal data for Human-Centered AI and Knowledge Graph-driven operational intelligence in renewable energy.

He is currently pursuing a Doctorate in Business Administration (expected in September 2027), with his dissertation in progress. He holds a Master's Degree from MIT, Pune and a D.Mech Engineering from Sou. Venutai Chavana / Sinhagad institutes. He also has certifications in Machine Learning and Data Science from L'avenir Institute and Analytics Vidhya, and studied Mathematics for Artificial Intelligence at Bhaskaracharya Pratishthan (covering Linear Algebra, Multivariate Calculus, Probability, and Discrete Math).
